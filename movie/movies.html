Movies to watch

  • RRR

    Director: S.S. Rajamouli

    Cast: NTR Jr., Ram Charan, Alia Bhatt, Ajay Devgn

    Description: "RRR" is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language period action drama film directed by S.S. Rajamouli. The movie stars NTR Jr., Ram Charan, Alia Bhatt, and Ajay Devgn in lead roles and is set in the pre-independence era, showcasing the fictionalized tale of two freedom fighters, Komaram Bheem and Alluri Sitarama Raju.
